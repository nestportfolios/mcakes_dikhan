5S Kitchen Methodology

Kalsoom champions the Japanese 5S framework — the gold standard for productive, safe, and efficient kitchens.

1S
Sort · Seiri
Remove unnecessary items from the kitchen. Keep only what's needed for cake production, eliminating clutter and confusion.
2S
Set in Order · Seiton
Organize ingredients, tools, and equipment for efficient cake making. Label and place items in designated, logical spots.
3S
Shine · Seiso
Clean and sanitize the kitchen, equipment, and tools after each use. Maintain a hygienic and safe environment at all times.
4S
Standardize · Seiketsu
Follow standard operating procedures (SOPs) for cake making, cleaning, and organization across all team activities.
5S
Sustain · Shitsuke
Make 5S a daily habit. Participate in regular kitchen audits, maintain discipline, and ensure the safe, efficient, and productive MCakes kitchen never slips — this is Kalsoom's ultimate goal.
